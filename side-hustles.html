Side Hustle FAQs

How much time should I dedicate to a side hustle?

Start with 5-10 hours per week to test the waters. As you become more efficient and see results, you can scale up to 15-20 hours. The key is consistency - even 5 focused hours weekly can generate significant extra income over time.

Do I need to pay taxes on side hustle income?

Yes, all side hustle income is taxable. If you earn more than $400 net from self-employment, you'll need to file Schedule C and pay self-employment tax. Keep detailed records and set aside 25-30% of your earnings for taxes.

What's the best side hustle for complete beginners?

For complete beginners, gig economy apps (DoorDash, Uber Eats) or simple local services (pet sitting, house cleaning) are great starting points. They require minimal skills, have immediate income potential, and let you test the waters with low commitment.

How do I balance a side hustle with a full-time job?

Start with time-blocking - dedicate specific hours weekly to your side hustle. Protect your rest time and set boundaries. Use weekends efficiently and consider waking up an hour earlier. The key is consistency, not massive time commitments.
