Savings Goal FAQs

Where should I keep my savings for short-term goals?

For goals within 5 years, use high-yield savings accounts or money market accounts. They offer better interest rates than regular savings while keeping your money accessible.

What if I can't save the calculated amount?

Adjust your timeline or goal amount. Even saving smaller amounts consistently is better than not saving at all. Consider ways to increase income or reduce expenses.

Should I pause retirement savings for other goals?

Generally no, especially if you get employer matching. Continue retirement savings while working on other goals, even if at a reduced rate during intense savings periods.

How do I prioritize multiple savings goals?

Focus on emergency fund first, then high-priority goals (down payment, debt repayment), then wants (vacation, car). Consider splitting your savings across multiple goals.
