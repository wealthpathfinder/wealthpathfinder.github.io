Utilization Recommendations

Ideal Range: Keep your overall utilization below 30% for the best credit score impact.
Individual Cards: Try to keep each card below 30% utilization as well.
Payment Strategy: Consider making multiple payments throughout the month to keep reported balances low.
Credit Limit Increases: Requesting higher limits can improve your utilization ratio (if you don't increase spending).

Quick Tips to Improve Utilization

Pay Down Balances

Focus on paying down cards with the highest utilization rates first

Time Your Payments

Make payments before your statement closing date to lower reported balances

Request Limit Increases

Ask for credit limit increases on existing cards (if you have good payment history)
