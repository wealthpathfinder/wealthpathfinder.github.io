Your 30-Day Emergency Fund Action Plan

Week 1: Calculate & Plan

  • Calculate monthly essential expenses
  • Set your target (3-6 months)
  • Open a high-yield savings account

Week 2: Find Extra Money

  • Review last month's spending
  • Identify 2-3 expenses to reduce
  • Sell $200 worth of unused items

Week 3: Automate

  • Set up automatic transfer
  • Start with $1,000 mini fund goal
  • Tell someone about your goal for accountability
