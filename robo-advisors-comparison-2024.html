Platform Overview

Betterment pioneered the robo-advisor space and remains a leader with its user-friendly interface and comprehensive feature set. The platform excels at goal-based investing, allowing you to separate money for retirement, emergency fund, or specific purchases.

Frequently Asked Questions

Are robo-advisors safe and secure?

Yes. Reputable robo-advisors use bank-level encryption (256-bit SSL) and are SIPC-insured up to $500,000 (similar to traditional brokerages). Your investments are held with a custodian, not the robo-advisor itself.

How do robo-advisors make money?

Most charge an annual management fee (0.15%-0.50% of assets). Some earn revenue from cash sweep programs, securities lending, or premium service tiers. Always check the fee schedule before investing.

Can I lose money with a robo-advisor?

Yes, all investing involves risk. Robo-advisors don't guarantee profits or protect against losses. However, their diversified portfolios are designed to manage risk according to your chosen risk level.

When should I NOT use a robo-advisor?

Consider traditional advice if you: have complex tax situations, need estate planning, own a business, require specialized strategies, or have over $500,000 in investable assets where customized service may be beneficial.
