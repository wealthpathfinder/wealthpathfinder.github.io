Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it take to get your first freelance client?

Most beginners land their first client within 2-3 weeks if they follow a systematic approach. The key is consistencyâ€”apply to 3-5 projects daily, send personalized proposals, and be willing to start with smaller projects to build your portfolio.

What should I charge with no experience?

Start with $15-25/hour or $100-300 per project for your first 3-5 clients. As you gain testimonials and build your portfolio, gradually increase your rates. Remember, your early goal isn't maximum incomeâ€”it's building credibility.

Do I need a website to start freelancing?

No, not initially. You can start with profiles on freelance platforms like Upwork and Fiverr. Create a simple LinkedIn profile showcasing your services. After landing your first 2-3 clients, then invest in a basic portfolio website.
