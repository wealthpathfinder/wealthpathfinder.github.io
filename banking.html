Banking FAQs

What's the difference between APY and interest rate?

APY (Annual Percentage Yield) includes compound interest, while the interest rate doesn't. Always compare APY when choosing savings accounts because it shows your actual earnings.

Are online banks safe?

Yes, reputable online banks are FDIC-insured just like traditional banks. They often have better security measures and higher rates due to lower overhead costs.

How much should I keep in my emergency fund?

Most experts recommend 3-6 months of essential expenses. Start with a $1,000 starter fund, then build up to your full target amount over time.

What happens if my bank fails?

FDIC insurance covers up to $250,000 per depositor, per bank. Your money is protected as long as you're within the insurance limits at an FDIC-member bank.
