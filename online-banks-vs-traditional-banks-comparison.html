How to Switch Banks: 5-Step Process

Step 1: Choose Your New Bank (1-2 hours)

Research and select an online bank for savings (like Ally, Discover, or Capital One 360) and/or a traditional bank for checking based on your needs.

Step 2: Open New Accounts (30 minutes)

Complete online applications for your new accounts. You'll need your Social Security number, driver's license, and current bank account information.

Step 3: Transfer Initial Funds (2-3 business days)

Move a small amount ($25-100) to test the transfer process. Once confirmed, transfer the bulk of your savings.

Step 4: Update Automatic Payments (1-2 hours)

Change your direct deposit information with your employer and update any automatic bill payments to your new accounts.

Step 5: Close Old Accounts (Optional)

Once all transactions have cleared and you're comfortable with the new setup, you can close old accounts to simplify your finances.

Frequently Asked Questions

Are online banks really FDIC insured?

Yes, absolutely. Legitimate online banks are FDIC insured just like traditional banks. You can verify any bank's FDIC status using the FDIC's BankFind tool. This insurance protects your money up to $250,000 per depositor, per bank.

How do I deposit cash with an online bank?

This is the main limitation of online banks. Options include: using a traditional bank checking account for cash deposits then transferring, using retail locations that accept cash deposits for certain online banks, or using money orders (though these have fees).

Can I get a mortgage or car loan from an online bank?

Yes, and often with better rates. Many online banks offer competitive rates on mortgages, auto loans, and personal loans. However, the process is entirely online, which some people find less personal than working with a local loan officer.

What happens if the online bank has technical issues?

Like any technology, online banking platforms can experience occasional downtime. Reputable online banks have 24/7 customer support and multiple ways to access your accounts (mobile app, website, phone banking). Most issues are resolved within hours.

Final Recommendation

üéØ For Most People: Hybrid Approach

üíª For Tech-Savvy Users: Full Online

üè¶ For Cash-Heavy Users: Traditional with Online Savings
