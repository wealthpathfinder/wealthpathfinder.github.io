Review FAQs

How do you maintain objectivity in your reviews?

We purchase and test products with our own funds whenever possible. We never accept payment for positive reviews and clearly disclose any affiliate relationships. Our testing methodology is standardized across all products to ensure fair comparisons.

How often do you update your reviews?

We review and update our content quarterly, or whenever significant changes occur (fee changes, new features, etc.). Each review clearly displays its "Last Updated" date, and we monitor products continuously for important updates.

Do you earn commissions from product recommendations?

Yes, we may earn affiliate commissions when readers sign up for products through our links. However, this never influences our ratings or recommendations. We frequently recommend products that don't offer affiliate commissions when they're the best option for our readers.

How do you choose which products to review?

We focus on products that are most relevant to our audience - typically market leaders, rising competitors, and options that serve specific needs or niches. Reader requests and market trends also influence our review schedule.
